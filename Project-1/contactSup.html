Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs):

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by visiting our Order Page and following the instructions provided.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept gold, silver, and bank notes etc. For more details, please refer to our payment information on the Order Page.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging. Please contact our support team for assistance with returns.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only ship within North America. International shipping is not available at this time.

I don't know how to properly use my device!

Please refer to the user manual included with your purchase. You may also refer to this user manual here Carbide Lamp Operating Guide. For additional assistance, contact our support team.
